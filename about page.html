About

Hannah, Chakrika and Anum have worked together to create a website on chocolate and its' history. The website also includes quiz to test your knowledge of the history of chocolate. The quiz relates to the timeline, which can be found on the homepage. There are five questions so you better try to remember the important facts so you can get 100%!

Chocolate is an essential part of all of our lives - it is there for us through our good days, the bad days, even just the days where everything is going okay.

Chocolate is incredibly versatile and you can make anything from the basic (but delicious) chocolate cakes to even chocolate sculptures. One thing we all enjoy is a bit of baking and chocolate can be our best friend during this process. A favourite of ours is chocolate brownies - they are so rich, heavenly just simply delicious.
